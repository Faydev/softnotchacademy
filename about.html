SOFTNOTCH ACADEMY

Softnotch Academy is a tech academy with the goal of making advanced IT knowledge affordable and accessible to the Nigerian youths.

We adopted a social and hands-on project based learning technique, offering a practical edge in an ultrate-modern, tech friendly learning environmennt that enhances student assimilation and retention. Softnotch reckons that with the sudden surge of tech start-ups all over the world, the need for tech skills has become a necessity. In respect to Nigeria's growing unemployment, Softnotch aims at contributing to the reduction of unemployment by equipping the youths with tech skills to compete favourably for tech jobs accross the globe.

Our Mission is to make Nogeria the No. 1 IT coutry in Africa in the next 5 years and top 5 in the world via training over 10,000 students.
